DR: Got it. Any history of asthma, heart problems, or clots?
PT: No asthma. My father had a DVT, I think.
DR: Medications?
PT: I take lisinopril for blood pressure, and I'm on a daily aspirin.
DR: Any smoking history?
PT: I quit about ten years ago. Smoked for twenty years before that.
DR: I want to check your oxygen saturation and listen to your lungs.
